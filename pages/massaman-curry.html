Massaman Curry

Description

Delicious Thai Beef Stew with a really creamy base and full of wonderful flavors

    Ingredients
  • 1 Can of Coconut Milk
  • 1 tbsp Curry Paste
  • 1 Cinnamon Stick
  • 3 Green or 2 black Cardamom Pods
  • 1 Star Anise Pod
  • 1.5 Lbs Beef Chuck
  • 1 Cup Water
  • 2 Cups Brown Rice
  • 1 lb Yukon Gold Potatoes
