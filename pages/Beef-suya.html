Beef Suya

Home

Beef suya is a heavily spiced sweet/sour skewer.

    Ingredients
  • 1/2 cup dry roasted peanuts
  • 1 tbsp sweet paprika
  • 1 tbsp ground ginger
  • 1 tbsp garlic powder
  • 1 tbsp onion powder
  • 2 tsp packed light brown sugar
  • 1 tsp cayenne pepper
  • Salt and Pepper
  • 3 tbsp grapeseed or other neutral oil
  • 1.5 lb flatiron steak, sliced into 1/2 inch strips
  • 1 tbsp lime juice, lime wedges
  1. Combine peanuts paprika, ginger, onion, garlic, sugar, cayenne, 3/4 tsp salt, 1 tsp pepper in food processor
  2. Reserve 1/3 cup of seasoning. Set aside.
  3. In large bowl combine seasoning with oil to form a paste
  4. In large bowl combine beef with 1 tsp salt. Toss to evenly coat.
  5. Add past to bowl with beef. Toss to evenly coat, massage into meat.
  6. Pack strips onto 12 inch skewers
  7. Place skewers on wire rack on top of foil lined rimmed baking sheet
  8. Heat with broiler, about 5 minutes, flipping after 2.5 minutes.
  9. Transfer to plate and let rest for 5 minutes
  10. Brush with lime juice. Sprinkle with rest of seasoning.
  11. Serve with lime wedges
