Come on, you know what a taco is

Good ol' quick and reliable Tacos. Soft or Hard shell, can't go wrong. Can even make them with tofu or fish for variety. Also, you can just microwave the beef if you're looking for that dry rubbery taco-bell beef.

Ingredients

  • 8 Soft or 12 Hard Tacos
  • 2 lbs Ground Beef
  • Taco Seasoning
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Shredded Cheddar Cheese
  • Sour Cream
  • Mild/Hot Sauce

Couple quick steps

  1. Add 1 tbsp oil to pan at med/high until shimmering
  2. Add and brown beef for 5-7 minutes
  3. Drain most of the fat/oil
  4. Add Taco seasoning, stir and cook for another 5 minutes
  5. Roughly chop lettuce, tomatoes, onions
  6. Heat up tacos. Or don't.
  7. Put everything else on table for easy serving and enjoy!
