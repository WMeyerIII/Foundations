Massaman Curry

Home

Seriously too good to make light of. Try it, you'll see.

Delicious Thai Beef Stew with a really creamy base full of wonderful flavors

Ingredients

  • High Smoke Point Oil
  • 1 Can of Coconut Milk
  • 1 tbsp Curry Paste
  • 1 Cinnamon Stick
  • 3 Green or 2 black Cardamom Pods
  • 1 Star Anise Pod
  • 2 tbsp Brown Sugar
  • 2 Tbsp Fish Sauce
  • 1.5 Lbs Beef Chuck
  • 1 Cup Water
  • 2 Cups Brown Rice
  • 1 lb Yukon Gold Potatoes
  • 1/4 of roasted peanuts

Straightforward, whole lot of waiting.

  1. Start rice, in cooker or by direction on box
  2. Add 2 tbsp Oil to Dutch Oven/Large pot on med-high heat until shimmering
  3. Add 1/2 cup from Can of Coconut milk, 1/4 cup Curry Paste, fry for 3-4 minutes
  4. While frying, cut up potatoes into 1-inch cubes
  5. Add rest of Can of Coconut Milk, 1 Cup of water.
  6. Mix together Coconut milk and paste. Add Cardamom Pods, Star Anise, Cinnamon Sticks, Brown Sugar, and Fish Sauce.
  7. Add Beef
  8. Simmer on med for 45 Minutes
  9. Remove Cardamom Pods, Star Anise, and cinnamon. This is very important, leaving them in can ruin the taste and texture.
  10. Add potatoes. Simmer for 20 minutes.
  11. Remove from heat. Add peanuts
